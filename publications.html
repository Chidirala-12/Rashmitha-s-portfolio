Book Chapter: Analysing Citizen Acceptance of AI-Driven Green Transportation

Lecture Notes in Intelligent Transportation and Infrastructure (LNITI) series

Co-authored a book chapter titled "Analysing Citizen Acceptance of AI-Driven Green Transportation: Mixed-Method Approach of Insights and Strategies for Enhancing Adoption." It focuses on the importance of citizen acceptance in adopting AI-driven green transportation systems for a better future and a safer environment. This research examined key factors like safety, security, cost efficiency, and convenience through a mixed-method approach, including online surveys of diverse demographics, focus groups, and real-world analytics. This also provides user-centric solutions.
